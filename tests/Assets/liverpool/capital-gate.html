          On your doorstep

          Social scenes

          Capital Gate is located 10 minutes from Liverpool city centre, in the London Road area. The property is very close to the University of Liverpool, LJMU and Liverpool Hope campuses, with supermarkets and shops nearby. There are plenty of student-friendly pubs, restaurants and takeaways on London Road – two minutes from Capital Gate.
          Local shops and restaurants include Branded Bargains, Lidl, TJ Hughes, Tesco, Subway, Dominos Pizza and a Chinese supermarket.
          The property is also five minutes from Liverpool Guild of Students, Brownlow Hill (UoL) and 20 minutes from Liverpool Students’ Union, Maryland Street (LJMU) which host a programme of student activities including society meetings and club nights.
          Liverpool city centre – 10 minutes’ walk away – boasts a wide range of shopping centres such as St Johns Centre and the Met Quarter, plus hundreds of restaurants and a bustling night life, particularly in popular areas such as Concert Square and Matthew Street.

          What's around?

          Capital Gate is 10 minutes from Liverpool city centre where there are a number of fantastic entertainment venues, including an Odeon iMAX cinema based at the Liverpool One shopping centre.
          Liverpool Empire Theatre is located next to Liverpool Lime Street train station. It has a large variety of concerts and stage productions at student-friendly prices. The Royal Court Theatre, just next to Queen Square bus station, is also home to stage productions as well as regular comedy acts.
          Liverpool Echo Arena is situated on the Kings Dock – roughly half an hour on foot from Capital Gate – among beautiful dock buildings renovated into clubs, restaurants and shops. It plays host to a variety of sporting, music and comedy events.
          There are many attractions based on arguably Liverpool’s most famous export, The Beatles. These include the Beatles Festival in August, plus attractions like The Cavern, The Beatles Story and Taxi Tours.

          Getting active

          The Sports Centre of the University of Liverpool is less than 10 minutes’ walk away. There are also a number of ‘Lifestyles’ gyms locally where members can visit a number of gyms across the city with a single membership.
          The nearest gym at Wavertree has an Olympic swimming pool and extensive playing fields. Everton Park Sports Centre has a swimming pool and outdoor football pitches for hire, and there is a Power League five-a-side football complex close by.
          Liverpool and Everton football clubs are separated only by Stanley Park, less than three miles from the city centre. Liverpool play their games at Anfield stadium, and Everton play at Goodison Park.
          Both stadiums are just a short bus ride away and offer stadium tours as well as match day tickets.

          Close and convenient

          Travel and transport

          There is a bus stop on London Road near to Capital Gate which takes you into the city centre and other routes into the north of Liverpool. The main buses for the university campuses outside the city centre are the 75, 76 and 78.  These run regularly and go from bus stops at Lime Street, Queen Square and right outside the property on London Road.
          It is easy to get about in Liverpool, buses run regularly and there are plenty of taxis. Capital Gate is in a central location, equidistant from the city centre, LJMU and UoL. There is also a National Express coach station on Norton Street, five minutes’ walk from Capital Gate.
          Liverpool Lime Street is the main train station for local and national rail services and is a gateway to the rest of the UK.  Manchester and Chester are only an hour away and Blackpool is only 90 minutes on the train. There are train and admission deals available to attractions around the country such as Alton Towers and Blackpool Pleasure Beach.

          Local essentials

          Capital Gate is next to a Lidl supermarket with a small Tesco express five minutes away. A larger ASDA supermarket is on Smithdown road, a short taxi or bus journey away.
          The city has various shopping centres such as Liverpool One, the Met Quarter, and Clayton Square providing an excellent choice of shops including John Lewis, Debenhams, Top Shop, All Saints, and Next.
          Local banks are situated on the University of Liverpool campus, 10 minutes away on Oxford Street, and Brownlow Hill – these include Barclays, HSBC, Natwest and Lloyds TSB. There is also a free cashpoint inside CostCutter, behind Lidl, and outside Tesco on London Road.
          Capital Gate is five minutes from UoL Harold Cohen Library for Medicine, Science and Engineering Subjects, and 10 minutes from Sydney Jones Library for Languages and Humanities subjects. It is also 10 minutes from LJMU Aldham Robarts Library on Maryland Street near the Student Union, LJMU Avril Robarts Library is on Tithebarn Street, a 25-minute walk. There is also the newly renovated Liverpool Central Library near Lime Street station.

          Your wellbeing

          Capital Gate’s proximity to the Liverpool University campus means that it is close to the healthcare and welfare facilities provided by the university. LJMU welfare services are located in the Marybone area on Tithebarn street.
          Student Health is based in the centre of UoL campus, where all students can register for healthcare. It is part of the Brownlow Group of surgeries, with other practices available around the city centre. The nearest of these is at the end of Daulby Street almost next to Capital Gate.
          UoL Student Services for welfare advice is based on the other side of Student Health, near the Guild of Students. LJMU welfare and advice centre is on Tithebarn street.
          The Royal Liverpool University Hospital is situated on Prescot Street – directly opposite Capital Gate. There is also Great Charlotte Street Walk-in Centre near St Johns Centre in the city centre, which provides walk-in services for minor injuries and sexual health without appointment.

          Unite Students accommodation at Capital Gate

          Capital Gate is in a great location for students of all universities in Liverpool. It sits on the edge of University of Liverpool campus, just a short walk from Liverpool John Moores University (LJMU) and Liverpool Hope University. Some rooms are reserved for LJMU first year students who can book a room via the University.

          Capital Gate is in a popular student area with many shops nearby and just 10 minutes from Liverpool Lime Street train station with local and national travel links. Liverpool city centre is a 10-minute walk away with a great range of shops, bars and restaurants.
          The property has over 400 rooms with en-suite bathrooms, arranged in four to six bed flats with shared kitchen and living facilities, perfect for sharing with friends or meeting new people. There is also a limited number of studio flats which can be dual occupancy, perfect for more independent student living or student couples.
          As with all our student accommodation in Liverpool, the property offers Wi-Fi, contents insurance and all bills included in the price, so there are no unexpected costs to worry about.
