      On your doorstep

      Social scenes

      Lying within the heart of the university district, Cambridge Court has bars and shops within a two-minute walk. On the outskirts of Liverpool city centre, the property is within five minutes of Hope Street; Liverpool’s famous Georgian Quarter.
      The Font is a student bar attached to Cambridge Court, with diner-style food menu and plenty of games. The Cambridge pub is two minutes’ walk from Cambridge Court, and boasts a traditional English pub atmosphere and beer garden. Liverpool Students’ Union (LJMU) and Liverpool Guild of Students (University of Liverpool) are both a five-minute walk away and host a variety of student activities including society meetings and club nights.
      Liverpool city centre has a great range of shopping and entertainment venues, and is just a short walk from Cambridge Court. The Liverpool One shopping district has a cinema, crazy golf, restaurants and bars, and seasonal attractions like a pop-up beach with deckchairs and a funfair in the summer and an ice rink in the winter.

      What's around?

      Students at Cambridge Court are within walking distance of many great cultural venues. Liverpool has a huge selection of established and independent art galleries, most of which are free to enter, such as the Liverpool Tate, the Walker Art Gallery, and the Open Eye Gallery.
      The city centre is just 15 minutes’ walk from the property, and is home to the Odeon IMAX Cinema based in the Liverpool One shopping district.
      Liverpool Echo Arena is situated on the Kings Dock among beautiful dock buildings renovated into clubs, restaurants and shops. It plays host to a variety of sporting, music and comedy events.
      There are many attractions based on arguably Liverpool’s most famous export, The Beatles. These include the Beatles Festival in August, plus attractions like The Cavern, The Beatles Story and Taxi Tours.
      The brand new Everyman Theatre and Philharmonic Court are right on our doorstep, and in the city centre the Liverpool Empire Theatre, Royal Court Theatre, Playhouse, and Epstein Theatre give a wide variety of performances from touring West End productions to stand-up comedy tours.

      Getting active

      The nearest gym to the Student Village is the University of Liverpool’s Sport Centre, which has a pool and gym, and hosts many University sports associations and societies. Both university campuses are very close to the property so sports facilities are never far away.
      Liverpool has a wide range of gyms, 12 of which can be accessed under a single council gym membership. The closest of these – Picton Lifestyles – is just a short bus ride, or 30-minute walk, from Cambridge Court.
      Closer to the Student Village, the University of Liverpool and LJMU have a range of sports associations and facilities which students can use.
      Liverpool and Everton football clubs are separated only by Stanley Park, less than three miles from the city centre. Liverpool play their games at Anfield stadium, and Everton play at Goodison Park. Both stadiums are just a short bus ride away and offer stadium tours as well as match day tickets.

      Close and convenient

      Travel and transport

      Buses run right past the student village, with routes taking you through the city centre or out to Smithdown Road for access to large supermarkets. However, the great location of Cambridge Court means that you’re able to walk to most of the city’s most popular areas.
      Cambridge Court's proximity to both university campuses means that you can walk to all on-campus facilities including lecture theatres, libraries and sports facilities.
      Liverpool Lime Street train station is just 15 minutes’ walk from the Student Village, while Liverpool Central station is just 10 minutes away.

      Local essentials

      Cambridge Court, situated in the Student Village, has great access to local shops. There is a Tesco Express less than 10 minutes’ walk from the property, and a larger Tesco store 15 minutes away. Easy access to local bus routes means you can get to an ASDA superstore in just five minutes.
      There are a number of high street banks right outside the property, including HSBC, Barclays, Natwest and Lloyds TSB.

      Your wellbeing

      Student Support Services is within five minutes’ walk. Based in the Student Services Centre, the service provides students with advice, support and information on financial issues, disabilities, international student issues and general welfare.
      The Royal Liverpool University Hospital is a 15-minute walk through the university campus and has a 24-hour accident and emergency ward.
      The NHS walk-in centre is a 15-minute walk into the city centre and can be used for advice and medical assistance for non-emergency illnesses and ailments.
      Brownlow Health Group Practice is within a 10-minute walk and is open Monday–Friday, offering a variety of student health services.

      Unite Students accommodation at Cambridge Court

      Cambridge Court is part of our Student Village, at the heart of Liverpool’s university district. Just a couple of minutes’ walk to Liverpool John Moores and University of Liverpool campuses, the property is in the perfect location to make the most of your time in the city.

      Read More
      Each of the properties in the Student Village has its own style and character, so there’s something to suit all tastes. Cambridge Court has a bar and convenience store in the building, and is highly popular with students from Liverpool John Moores (LJMU), University of Liverpool, Liverpool Institute of Performing Arts (LIPA) and Edge Hill University. The property is just 15 minutes’ walk from Liverpool city centre.
      The property has a range of en-suite rooms with shared kitchen and living areas, perfect for sharing with friends or meeting new people. There are also three types of studios available, for more independent student living. This characterful property is Grade II listed, so every room is unique.
      As with all our Liverpool student village accommodation, Cambridge Court offers Wi-Fi, contents insurance and all utility bills included in the price, so there are no unexpected costs to worry about.
