    On your doorstep

    Social scenes

    Apollo Court is located on the edge of Liverpool city centre, in the London Road area. The property is very close to LJMU campuses, with supermarkets and shops nearby. There are plenty of student-friendly pubs, restaurants and takeaways on London Road – two minutes from Apollo Court.
    Local shops and restaurants include Branded Bargains, Lidl, TJ Hughes, Tesco, Subway, Dominos Pizza and a Chinese supermarket.
    The property is also 20 minutes from Liverpool Students’ Union, Maryland Street (LJMU) and 10 minutes from Liverpool Guild of Students Brownlow Hill (UoL), which hosts many student activities including society meetings and club nights.
    Liverpool city centre – five minutes’ walk away – boasts a wide range of shopping centres such as St Johns Centre and the Met Quarter, plus hundreds of restaurants and a bustling night life, particularly in popular areas such as Concert Square and Matthew Street.

    What's around?

    Apollo Court is five minutes from Liverpool city centre where there are a number of fantastic entertainment venues, including an Odeon iMAX cinema based at the Liverpool One shopping centre.
    Liverpool Empire Theatre is located next to Liverpool Lime Street train station. It has a large variety of concerts and stage productions at student-friendly prices. The Royal Court Theatre, just next to Queen Square bus station, is also home to stage productions as well as regular comedy acts.
    Liverpool Echo Arena is situated on the Kings Dock – roughly half-an-hour on foot from Apollo Court – among beautiful dock buildings renovated into clubs, restaurants and shops. It plays host to a variety of sporting, music and comedy events.
    There are many attractions based on arguably Liverpool’s most famous export, The Beatles. These include the Beatles Festival in August, plus attractions like The Cavern, The Beatles Story and Taxi Tours.

    Getting active

    The Sports Centre of the University of Liverpool is less than 10 minutes’ walk away. There are also a number of ‘Lifestyles’ gyms locally and members can visit a number of gyms across the city with a single membership. 
    The nearest gym at Wavertree has an Olympic swimming pool and extensive playing fields. Everton Park Sports Centre has a swimming pool and outdoor football pitches for hire, and there is a Power League five-a-side football complex close by.
    Liverpool and Everton football clubs are separated only by Stanley Park, less than three miles from the city centre. Liverpool play their games at Anfield stadium, and Everton play at Goodison Park.
    Both stadiums are just a short bus ride away and offer stadium tours as well as match day tickets.

    Close and convenient

    Travel and transport

    There is a bus stop on London Road next to Apollo Court which takes you into the city centre. The main buses for the university campuses outside the city centre are the 75, 76 and 78.  These run regularly and go from bus stops at Lime Street, Queen Square and right outside the property on London Road.
    It is easy to get about in Liverpool, buses run regularly and there are plenty of taxis. Apollo Court is in a central location, equidistant to the city centre, LJMU and UoL. There is also a National Express coach station on Norton Street, five minutes’ walk from Grand Central.
    Liverpool Lime Street is the main train station for local and national rail services and is a gateway to the rest of the UK.  Manchester and Chester are only one hour away and Blackpool is only 90 minutes on the train. There are train and admission deals available to attractions around the country, such as Alton Towers and Blackpool Pleasure Beach.

    Local essentials

    Apollo Court has a Tesco Express nearby, with a Lidl supermarket five minutes away. A larger ASDA supermarket is on Smithdown Road, a short taxi or bus journey away.
    The city has various shopping centres such as Liverpool One, the Met Quarter, and Clayton Square providing an excellent choice of shops including John Lewis, Debenhams, Top Shop, All Saints, and Next.
    Local banks are situated on the University of Liverpool campus, 10 minutes away on Oxford Street, and Brownlow Hill – these include Barclays, HSBC, Natwest and Lloyds TSB. There is also a free cashpoint inside CostCutter, behind Lidl, and outside Tesco on London Road.
    Apollo Court is 10 minutes from LJMU Aldham Robarts Library on Maryland Street near the Student Union, LJMU Avril Robarts Library is on Tithebarn Street, a 20-minute walk. There is also the newly renovated Liverpool Central Library near Lime Street station.

    Your wellbeing

    Apollo Court's proximity to the Liverpool University campus means that it is close to the healthcare and welfare facilities provided by the university. LJMU welfare services are located in the Marybone area on Tithebarn Street.
    Student Health is based in the centre of UoL campus, where all students can register for healthcare. It is part of the Brownlow Group of surgeries, with other practices available around the city centre. The nearest of these is at the end of Daulby Street.
    UoL Student Services for welfare advice is based on the other side of Student Health near the Guild of Students. LJMU welfare and advice centre is on Tithebarn street.
    The Royal Liverpool University Hospital is situated on Prescot Street – five minutes from Apollo Court. There is also Great Charlotte Street Walk-in Centre near St Johns Centre in the city centre, which provides walk-in services for minor injuries and sexual health without appointment.
